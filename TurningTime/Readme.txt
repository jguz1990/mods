Soul Filcher's Turning Time mod
By Soul Filcher, 2021.
Downloaded from Steam Workshop.

This mod adds some special zombies.


-----SPECIAL ZOMBIES----------------------------------------------------------------

-Banshee: These don't follow the player unless someone gets close to it.
-Nemesis: Don't let it get to you, don't let it follow you home!
